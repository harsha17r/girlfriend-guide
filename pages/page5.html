About This Guide

Girlfriend Guide started as a joke.
My (mostly female) friends kept giving me "boyfriend advice", so I ’d pretend to take notes. Then they actually asked for the list—to send to their boyfriends. In summer 2025, I wanted to bring my Figma designs to life, so I jumped in, explored new AI tools, played with visuals, obsessed over fonts, and rewrote the copy. This is what came out.
Just a playful experiment. Use responsibly.
